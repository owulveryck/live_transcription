üéØ Phrase Sets

Purpose: Improve recognition of specific words or phrases that are important to your use case.

How it works: Each phrase gets a boost value (0-20) that increases its recognition likelihood. Higher boost = more likely to be recognized.

When to use: For proper nouns, technical terms, brand names, or any words frequently misrecognized.

// Example phrase sets with boost values:
OpenAI:15 // High boost for important brand
machine learning:10 // Medium boost for common term
artificial intelligence:12
Google Cloud:8

üé® Custom Classes

Purpose: Create your own vocabulary sets for domain-specific terminology.

How it works: Group related terms together (like company names) with a shared boost value. All items in the class get the same recognition boost.

When to use: For categories of terms frequently mentioned together (competitors, products, locations, etc.).

ü§î Key Differences Explained

Overlap Example: "OpenAI" appears in both phrase sets AND custom classes because:

  • In Phrase Sets: Gets individual boost value (e.g., 15) as a specific important term
  • In Custom Classes: Gets grouped with other AI companies using shared boost value (e.g., 10)
  • Result: Both configurations apply, giving "OpenAI" extra recognition strength
